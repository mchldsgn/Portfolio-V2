POPPI

Anime inspired cosmetics.

POPPi is an upcoming beauty brand that draws inspiration from the world of anime and the cosplay community. With a commitment to originality, POPPi sets itself apart by crafting unique characters that shape their product lineup, steering clear of mainstream and popularized anime icons. Their aim is to foster a space where creativity thrives in the realm of makeup, while ensuring affordability remains at the forefront.

I collaborated with POPPi on packaging design for their Sakura Haze Eyeshadow Palette prototype, aiming for attention-grabbing and feminine packaging. We carefully selected fonts, included holographic elements, and vibrant colors to complement their logo for a captivating brand experience.
