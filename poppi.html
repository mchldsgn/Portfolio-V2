POPPI

Anime inspired cosmetics.

POPPI, an upcoming beauty brand draws inspiration from anime and the cosplay. With a commitment to originality, POPPI sets itself apart by crafting unique characters that shape their product lineup avoiding mainstream anime icons. Their goal is to nurture creativity in makeup without sacrificing affordability. We created a brand experience full of playful vintage-inspired designs which translated through to postcards, packaging, mailer boxes and more.

I spent time designing their Sakura Haze Eyeshadow Palette packaging and prototype.
The aim was to create a striking but feminine design.
