Gem Tours

As part of the Scrimba Front End Development Path, students were tasked with a project to build an information homepage for their hometown. As a Miami native, I was inspired to create a conceptual tour/travel agency site highlighting some of the most popular tourist attractions and my personal favorites. I performed a detailed analysis, comparing my design to top tourism websites featured on Skift to evaluate accessibility, user interface, and ease of use.
