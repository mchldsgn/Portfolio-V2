Gem Tours

As part of the Scrimba Front End Development Path, students were tasked with a project to build an information homepage for their hometown. As a Miami native, I was inspired to create a tour/travel agency site highlighting some of the most popular tourist spots (and my personal favs). I performed a detailed analysis to compare my design to the top tourism websites featured on Skift - accessibility, user interface, and ease of use were my evaluation points
