Tomoki & Friends is a conceptual children's toy and game brand designed to empower both adults and children. The concept revolves around fostering the development of essential skills, like math, time management and organization during a child's early years and up.

Drawing inspiration from anime and Scandi-Style children's interior design, I was motivated by the most commonly showcased Javascript projects featured in the portfolios of aspiring, junior, and new software developers/engineers. These projects receive labels such as "too basic" or "too beginner". Consequently, these portfolios from new developers are overlooked for professional software engineer/developer roles. To turn this around, I decided to embrace the simplicity of these projects and designed them to be more appealing to a younger audience.
